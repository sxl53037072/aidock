Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try AiDock for free?

Yes! AiDock's free tier is fully functional with support for ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. You can use folders, search, export, and more — no credit card required.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay through our payment partner Paddle.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

What happens to my data if I downgrade?

Your data is never deleted. If you exceed Free tier limits after downgrading, existing items are preserved but you won't be able to create new ones beyond the limits.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. See our refund policy for details.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. AiDock stores all data locally on your device. We do not read, transmit, or store your conversation content. See our privacy policy for details.

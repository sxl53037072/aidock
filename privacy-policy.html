AiDock Privacy Policy

Chrome Extension — AI Chat Organizer

Last updated: April 16, 2026

TL;DR — AiDock does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All conversation data stays on your device in Chrome's local storage. No analytics, no tracking, no external servers.

1. Information We Collect

None. AiDock does not collect any personal information, usage analytics, or telemetry of any kind.

2. How AiDock Works

AiDock reads only conversation titles from the sidebar of supported AI platforms to help you organize your chats. Specifically:

  • A content script runs on supported AI platform pages (listed below).
  • It reads conversation titles and URLs from the visible sidebar elements.
  • This data is stored locally in Chrome's storage on your device.
  • The extension does not read conversation content, messages, or any personal data.
  • The extension does not modify any page content on AI platforms.

4. Permissions Explained

PermissionPurpose
storageStores your preferences (theme, language, folder structure) and conversation metadata locally in Chrome's extension storage. Data never leaves your browser.
sidePanelDisplays the conversation management panel as a Chrome side panel.
activeTabReads the current tab's URL to identify which AI platform the user is on.
tabsNavigates to the correct conversation URL when user clicks a conversation in the panel.
Host permissionsInjects a content script on supported AI platform pages to read conversation titles from the sidebar. The script only reads publicly visible sidebar elements.

5. Network Requests

AiDock makes zero network requests. The extension operates entirely offline once installed. There are no API calls, no telemetry endpoints, and no external resource loading.

6. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device via Chrome's chrome.storage.local API:

  • Conversation metadata — titles, platform identifiers, folder assignments, pinned/starred status
  • Folder structure — folder names and organization
  • User preferences — theme (dark/light), language (English/Chinese), visible platforms

No conversation content or messages are stored. All data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

7. Third-Party Services

AiDock does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

8. Children's Privacy

AiDock does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page with the new date. Since AiDock is designed as a local-only tool, we do not anticipate significant changes.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
